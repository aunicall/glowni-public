Glowni Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Thank you for using Glowni (the "App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. We do not collect any personal information

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to: name, email, phone number, location data, IP address, contacts, photos, health data, device identifiers (IDFA / IDFV), usage behavior, crash logs, or analytics data.

2. Local Data Storage

All preferences you configure inside the App (such as selected color theme, sound, brightness, timer, volume, etc.) are stored locally on your device via UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device, is never uploaded to any server, and will be deleted together with the App when you uninstall it.

3. Network Requests

The App uses Apple's On-Demand Resources (ODR) technology to download audio files from Apple's CDN the first time you select a sound. This process is hosted entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. It does not involve any third-party servers, and we cannot track, log, or access your download behavior.

Other than ODR downloads, the App performs no other network communication.

4. Third-Party SDKs

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, including analytics tools, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting services, or social login providers.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children. As the App collects no personal data at all, there is no children's privacy concern.

6. Permissions

The App does not request any sensitive permissions: no location, camera, photo library, contacts, microphone, or push notification access. It only uses standard audio playback, which does not require additional user authorization.

7. Changes to This Policy

If the App introduces any feature in the future that may affect this Privacy Policy (e.g., accounts, in-app purchases, analytics), we will clearly notify you within the App and update this document accordingly.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: glowni@163.com

三、网络请求

四、第三方 SDK

本应用未集成任何第三方 SDK，包括分析工具、广告 SDK、崩溃上报工具以及社交登录。

五、儿童隐私

本应用不专门面向 13 岁以下儿童设计，也不会故意收集任何儿童的个人信息。由于本应用不收集任何个人信息，因此不存在儿童隐私问题。

六、权限使用说明

本应用不请求任何敏感权限：不请求位置、相机、相册、通讯录、麦克风、推送通知等权限。本应用仅使用标准的音频播放能力，这一能力无需您的额外授权。
