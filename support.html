Frequently Asked Questions

Why does a sound need to download the first time I tap it?

To keep the app install tiny, Glowni uses Apple's On-Demand Resources (ODR) system. Audio files are hosted on Apple's own CDN and are downloaded only when you first select them. Once downloaded, they stay on your device and play instantly the next time.

"Couldn't reach the cloud" — how do I fix it?

This friendly message appears when the first-time download fails. Please check your Wi-Fi or cellular connection, then tap the track again to retry. A few seconds of signal is usually enough — after the file is cached, no network is ever required again.

Does Glowni work offline?

Yes. After the first download of any sound, Glowni runs entirely offline. No account, no tracking, no network calls.

How do I unlock the Child Lock?

When Child Lock is active, all taps are blocked and a small lock hint appears. Press and hold anywhere on the screen for 3 seconds — a ring will fill around your finger. When the ring completes, the lock is released.

What does the sleep timer do when it ends?

In the final 60 seconds, audio fades out gently (logarithmic curve). When the timer reaches zero, the sound stops and the screen slowly dims to black to save battery. Tap the screen to wake it up again.

What exactly does Glowni Pro unlock?

A one-time purchase that adds 11 more light themes and 6 more sound categories (18 additional tracks) to the free tier. It's a one-time purchase with lifetime access — no subscription, no ads. The free tier (Amber light, Rain sound, Silence) remains fully usable without any purchase.

I bought Pro on another device / reinstalled the app. How do I get it back?

Open the paywall (tap any locked color or sound) and tap Restore Purchase at the bottom. As long as you're signed in with the same Apple ID that made the purchase, Pro will be restored instantly and for free.

Can I use Glowni with my own music or Spotify?

Glowni's audio session is set to mix with others, so you can start your favorite playlist in another app and use Glowni purely as a bedside lamp with the screen glow — or layer both audio sources together.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with the Apple ID used for the purchase. Refund eligibility is determined by Apple.

Does Glowni collect any data about me?

No. Glowni collects zero personal data. All of your settings (theme, volume, timer preference, etc.) are stored locally on your device via UserDefaults and never leave it. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

常见问题

为什么第一次点一首音效需要下载？

为了让 App 体积保持很小，Glowni 使用苹果的 按需资源（On-Demand Resources, ODR）技术。音频文件托管在 Apple 自己的 CDN 上，只在你第一次选中某类音效时下载。下载完成后会一直缓存在设备上，之后播放无需网络。

出现 "Couldn't reach the cloud" 怎么办？

这是首次下载时网络不佳的友好提示。请检查你的 Wi-Fi 或移动网络，然后再次轻点曲目重试。几秒的良好网络就够了——下载一次之后永远不再需要联网。

Glowni 可以离线使用吗？

可以。任一音效首次下载成功后，Glowni 完全离线运行：无账号、无追踪、无网络请求。

童锁怎么解锁？

童锁开启后，所有轻触都会被拦截并弹出小锁图标提示。在屏幕任意位置长按 3 秒——手指周围会出现一个填充圆环，环满即解锁。

计时器结束后会发生什么？

最后 60 秒，音频按对数曲线缓慢淡出。计时归零时声音停止，屏幕也会慢慢变黑以省电。再次轻点屏幕即可唤醒。

Glowni Pro 具体解锁了什么？

一次性购买，新增 11 种光色主题与 6 类助眠音效（18 首曲目）。一次购买，终身使用；无订阅，无广告。免费层（Amber 光色、Rain 雨声、Silence 静音）始终可用，无需任何购买。

在别的设备买过 / 重装过 App，怎么恢复？

点任意加锁的光色或音效，打开付费墙，拉到底部点击 Restore Purchase。只要登录的是当初购买时的同一个 Apple ID，Pro 会立即免费恢复。

可以和 Apple Music / Spotify 一起用吗？

可以。Glowni 的音频会话设为 "mix with others"（与其他音频混合），你完全可以先在其他 app 播放你喜欢的歌单，然后把 Glowni 当作床头灯使用——或者把两种声音叠加在一起。

怎么申请退款？

所有购买都由苹果官方处理。请访问 reportaproblem.apple.com，用当时购买所用的 Apple ID 登录后提交退款申请。是否符合退款条件由苹果判定。

Glowni 会收集我的什么数据？

不会收集任何个人数据。你的偏好（主题、音量、计时器等）全部以 UserDefaults 存在本机，从不上传。详见 隐私政策。
